Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our service. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our users. The information you enter (Bundle ID or Store ID) is used solely for the purpose of querying Apple's public iTunes Search API and is never logged or stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our service communicates directly with Apple's iTunes Search API to retrieve app information. We do not use any other third-party services, such as analytics or advertising networks, that would track your activity.

Cookies

We do not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.
